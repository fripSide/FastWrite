\section{Evaluation}
To validate the effectiveness of FastWrite, we conducted a user study with 12 Ph.D. students from top universities.
Participants refined a draft abstract using both FastWrite and a baseline editor.

\subsection{Quantitative Results}
We measured task completion time and the number of accepted AI-generated suggestions.

        \midrule

        Acceptance Rate (\%) & N/A & $78\%$ \\
        User Satisfaction (1-5) & $3.4$ & $\mathbf{4.7}$ \\
        \bottomrule

\subsection{Qualitative Feedback}
Users praised the \textit{Refine} mode for its ability to sound like a native speaker while preserving technical accuracy.
One participant noted: "It's like having a senior professor review my work, but without the pressure."
